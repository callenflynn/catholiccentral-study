Enzymes

Proteins that speed up biological reactions by lowering activation energy.

How enzymes work:

  1. Substrates (reactants) bind to the enzyme's active site
  2. Substrates fit like puzzle pieces
  3. Reaction occurs (often breaking down large molecules)
  4. Enzyme can be reused

Key terms:

  • Substrate: The reactants in an enzyme reaction
  • Active site: Specific binding location on the enzyme
